WHO WE ARE

About CoSACSA

Our Story

CoSACSA Gauteng is the provincial branch of the College Sports, Arts and Culture South Africa. We are committed to promoting sports, arts, and culture within the TVET sector.

Our Mission

To create platforms that enable TVET college students to showcase their talents, develop their skills, and build meaningful connections.
